OUR
MISSION

Volunteer Here connects the community to different opportunities with local organizations and businesses. We aim to make participating in community service more accessible, and increase outreach about different positions and roles. Based in Montour County, we currently only serve the towns of Danville, Bloomsburg, and Lewisburg, but hope to expand with more experience and input.
