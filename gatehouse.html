ABOUT:

The Gate House provides a safe shelter for homeless individuals and families. They also offer services to help make the transition back to independence quicker and easier. This includes aid with obtaining jobs, finding housing, empowering education, and learning important life skills. Volunteers can help by fundraising, handling clerical work, taking care of the building, and more.
