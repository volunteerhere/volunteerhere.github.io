Volunteer Here Website Accessibility Policy



At Volunteer Here, we strive for accessibility. We want to ensure that everyone, including persons with disabilities, has full and equal access to our content.

We welcome any feedback on your experience with volunteerhere.neocities.org. Email us at volunteerherepa@gmail.com with any questions or comments.

Our website complies with ADA requirements as listed here.

Volunteerhere.neocities.org is compatible with most Internet browsers including Chrome, FireFox, and Safari. However, it may not perform optimally on Internet Explorer and older browser versions.

All accessibility features were coordinated and incorporated by Abril Xu.

For more information on web accessibility and legal requirements, look into these resources: ADA.gov, Section508.gov, Accessible.org, and W3.org/WAI.
